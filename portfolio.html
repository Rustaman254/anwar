Pesabits

Pesabits is a decentralized peer-to-peer lending platform revolutionizing access to financial services through blockchain technology. By connecting borrowers and lenders directly, Pesabits enables users to secure fiat loans using cryptocurrency as collateral, eliminating the need to sell valuable digital assets. The platform matches borrowers with lenders in minutes, offering flexible loan terms and competitive interest rates. With a robust rating system to promote trust and accountability, Pesabits ensures secure, transparent transactions via smart contracts. In the event of a 60-day borrower default, Pesabits compensates lenders, enhancing platform reliability. Designed for global accessibility, Pesabits empowers users to unlock liquidity while fostering financial inclusion in the evolving DeFi landscape.
